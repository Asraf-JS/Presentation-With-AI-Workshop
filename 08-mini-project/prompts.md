# 08 — Mini Project: Prompts

Use these throughout the project to accelerate each stage. Work through them in sequence — they follow the same order as the project steps.

---

## Step 1 — Diagnosing the deck

```
I am about to improve a corporate PowerPoint deck. Before I start, help me diagnose it. Here are the slide titles in order: [paste your titles]. Tell me: (1) what the likely key message is, (2) where the narrative is weakest, (3) which slides are probably redundant, and (4) what I should fix first.
```

---

## Step 2 — Restructuring

```
Here is my current slide sequence: [paste titles or brief descriptions]. I want to restructure this around the message: "[your key message]". Suggest a revised slide order using the Situation-Complication-Resolution arc. Keep the same content — just change the order and flag anything I should cut.
```

---

## Step 3 — Rewriting titles and content

```
Here are all my slide titles: [paste titles]. Rewrite every title that is a label rather than a point. Keep titles under 12 words. Do not change titles that already make a clear assertion.
```

```
Here is a slide with too many bullet points: [paste title and bullets]. Cut this down to the 3 most important points for a senior leadership audience. Rewrite each retained point to be one short sentence under 10 words.
```

---

## Step 4 — Data slides

```
Here is a data slide: Title: "[your title]", Content: [describe the chart or table]. (1) Does the title make a point or just label the data? (2) Is the chart type appropriate for what is being shown? (3) What should I cut or highlight to make the insight clearer?
```

---

## Step 5 — Canva brief

```
I need to build a cover slide in Canva for a quarterly review presentation. The deck is for Nusantara Capital's strategy department. Presentation title: "Q3 2025 Departmental Review". Audience: CEO and division heads. Visual style: professional, corporate, minimal. Colour palette: navy #1E2761 and teal #028090. Write a brief I can use to guide my Canva design work.
```

---

## Step 6 — Consistency check

```
I have finished my deck. It uses PowerPoint for 12 slides and has 3 Canva-designed slides inserted as PNG images. Give me a 7-point visual consistency checklist I should work through before the final review.
```

---

## Step 7 — AI review of the final deck

```
Here are my final slide titles in order: [paste titles]. (1) Can you identify the key message from the titles alone? (2) Are there any titles that are still unclear or passive? (3) Does the sequence follow a logical narrative arc? Give me specific suggestions for any titles that need work.
```

---

## Preparing for peer presentation

```
I am about to present my improved deck to a small group for peer feedback. The presentation is 8 minutes. Help me prepare: (1) a 60-second opening that states the key message upfront, (2) two transition phrases to move between the data section and the recommendation section, and (3) a one-sentence closing statement that makes it clear what I need the audience to take away.
```
