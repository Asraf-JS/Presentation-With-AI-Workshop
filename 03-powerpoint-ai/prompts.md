# 03 — PowerPoint AI Features: Prompts

Use these in **ChatGPT, Copilot Chat, or any AI chat tool** alongside PowerPoint.

---

## Rewriting slide titles

```
Here are the slide titles from my quarterly review deck. Rewrite each one so it states a point rather than just labelling a topic. Keep each title under 12 words.

1. Q3 Revenue Summary
2. Project Status
3. Team and Headcount
4. Key Risks and Issues
5. Q4 Outlook
```

```
My slide title is "Department Performance Overview". The slide contains: revenue above target, two project delays, one new client win, headcount stable. Write 3 alternative titles that each emphasise a different angle of this information.
```

---

## Trimming bullet point content

```
Here is the content of one of my slides. Rewrite the bullet points so each one is a single sentence of no more than 10 words. Remove anything that the title already covers. Do not add new information.

Title: Revenue exceeded target for the second quarter running
Bullets:
- Total Q3 revenue came in at RM 4.2 million against a planned target of RM 3.8 million
- This represents a positive variance of approximately 10.5% above plan
- The performance was driven primarily by three new client engagements which were onboarded in July and August
- In addition to revenue, client satisfaction scores remained high throughout the quarter
```

```
This slide has too many bullet points. I have 9 items. Help me cut it down to the 3 most important points for a senior leadership audience that needs to make a Q4 budget decision.

[paste your 9 bullet points]
```

---

## Writing speaker notes

```
Write speaker notes for this PowerPoint slide. 

Title: Two projects are at risk of missing Q4 milestones
Bullets:
- Project Helang: delayed 3 weeks due to vendor issues
- Project Rimau: resource gap since August resignation

Context: The presenter is a Strategy department head speaking to the CEO. This is a quarterly review. Tone should be direct — acknowledge the risk clearly without being defensive. The next slide proposes a solution. Time on this slide: about 2 minutes.
```

```
I have written speaker notes for my slides but they are too long — I tend to write full sentences and end up reading from the screen. Here are my notes for one slide: [paste notes]. Condense them into 4 short talking points, each one a reminder phrase rather than a full sentence.
```

## Working with PowerPoint Designer

Use these in an AI tool before or after using Designer in PowerPoint:

```
I have a slide with this content: one large number (RM 4.2M), a short label ("Q3 Revenue"), and one supporting line ("10% above target"). PowerPoint Designer is suggesting 6 different layouts. Describe what kind of layout would be most appropriate for this type of slide and why.
```

```
PowerPoint Designer keeps suggesting layouts with stock images and decorative icons for my data slides. I want clean, minimal slides. What settings or approaches should I use to get simpler layout suggestions?
```
