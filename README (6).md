# 02 — Structuring Your Message: Prompts

Use these in **ChatGPT, Copilot Chat, or any AI chat tool** before opening PowerPoint.

---

## Defining audience and purpose

```
I am preparing a quarterly review presentation for senior leadership at a Malaysian investment holding company. The audience includes the CEO and two division heads. They need to make a resource allocation decision by end of month. Help me identify: (1) what they care most about, (2) what objections they are likely to raise, and (3) what a successful outcome from this presentation looks like.
```

```
I have a presentation to give about my department's Q3 results. The purpose is to report progress, but I also need approval for additional budget in Q4. How should I structure the deck differently depending on whether the primary goal is reporting or requesting?
```

---

## Writing a key message

```
Here are my Q3 results: revenue RM 4.2M against a target of RM 3.8M, 3 new clients onboarded, 2 project delays, headcount stable. Write a single-sentence key message for a presentation to the CEO that captures what matters most. Give me 3 versions with different angles.
```

```
My presentation covers too many topics and I am struggling to find a single key message. Here are my 6 main points: [paste your points]. Help me identify which one should be the spine of the presentation and how the others should relate to it.
```

---

## Building a storyline

```
I need to present Q3 results to senior leadership and request approval for a resource reallocation. Use the Situation-Complication-Resolution structure to help me plan the narrative arc. Give me a suggested flow with 6-8 slide beats and a headline for each beat.
```

```
Here is my current slide order: [paste your agenda or slide titles]. Evaluate whether this follows a clear narrative arc. Identify where the story breaks down and suggest a reordered version.
```

---

## Data storytelling

```
I have a slide with this data: Q1 RM 3.1M, Q2 RM 3.6M, Q3 RM 4.2M, target was RM 3.8M for each quarter. The slide title is currently "Quarterly Revenue". Write me 3 alternative titles that tell a story instead of just labelling the data.
```

```
I have a table with 12 rows and 6 columns showing project status across all departments. I need to turn this into a single, clear insight for a leadership presentation. Help me identify the most important point in this data and suggest how to present it on one slide.
```

---

## Planning the deck before opening PowerPoint

```
I am about to build a 15-minute presentation for my department's quarterly review at Nusantara Capital. Before I open PowerPoint, help me fill in this planning template:

AUDIENCE: Strategy department leadership including CEO
PURPOSE: Report Q3 results and request Q4 resource approval
KEY MESSAGE: [help me write this based on our results — revenue above target, 2 project risks, 1 reallocation needed]

Give me a suggested storyline with 7-8 beats and rough slide count.
```
